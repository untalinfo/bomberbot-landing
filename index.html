School Manager

In Bomberbot School Manager you will have a simple panel in which you can access all its functionalities, which makes it much simpler, faster and easier to use.

  • Managing Teachers
  • Reports
  • Communications

Manage all your teachers and courses

Bomberbot did not have a platform in which schools could keep track of their teachers, their licenses and a direct means of communication, which is why Bomberbot School Manager arises

  • Create, update or delete a teacher
  • Consult teacher information
  • See a list of all teachers associated with your school

Get reports with one click

The generation of reports is from the beginning of development because it is one of the fundamental pillars to contribute to the solution sought by Bomberbot

  • Get a detailed report by teacher, where you can find your score, total students, number of lessons, etc.
  • You can have a general report of all teachers to have a more general view
  • You can print your reports to get their physical version.

Send mail from the application

Bomberbot School Manager has a section dedicated to sending messages to its teachers, you can access this function from the main panel.

  • Send emails easily
  • You can consult the mailing list associated with your school
  • You do not need to access your external mail server to send an email
